Key Financial Formulas

Gross Profit Margin

$$\text{Gross Profit Margin} = \frac{\text{Revenue} - \text{COGS}}{\text{Revenue}} \times 100\%$$

Earnings Per Share (EPS)

$$\text{EPS} = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Outstanding Shares}}$$
These are fundamental financial ratios used in our analysis. Gross margin tracks manufacturing efficiency and pricing power. EPS is the primary metric shareholders use to evaluate company performance.
